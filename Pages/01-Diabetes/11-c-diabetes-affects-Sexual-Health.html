How diabetes affects Sexual Health?

High blood sugar levels can cause nerve damage throughout the body.

In Women:
  • Decreased ability to experience sexual stimulation / arousal
  • Reduced release of vaginal lubricant due to reduced nerve sensitivity
  • Painful sex
  • Reduced ability to experience an orgasm
In Men:
  • Inability to achieve or maintain an erection (Erectile Dysfunction)
  • Performance Anxiety due to fear of sexual failure
  • Reduced Sex Drive
  • Decreased Libido
