HOW DO I KNOW IF I AM DRINKING TOO MUCH ALCOHOL?

If you do any of the following:

  • You believe you should reduce your alcohol consumption
  • Your drinking has drawn criticism from others
  • You feel bad or guilty about your drinking
  • You require a drink in the morning to calm your nerves or recover from your hangover.

If any of the following apply to someone you know, they may be misusing alcohol:

  • They frequently consume more than 14 units of alcohol per week, and because of their excessive drinking
  • They occasionally forget what happened the night before
  • They fail to fulfil their obligations as a result of their drinking (for instance, by skipping work or an appointment because they're inebriated or hungover)
