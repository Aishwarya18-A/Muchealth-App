HELPING YOU CHOOSE LESS SATURATED FAT

To assist you in reducing the overall fat content of your diet:

  • When you are shopping, remember to compare food labels to choose foods with less fat. Choose dairy substitutes with less or no fat
  • Before preparing meat and poultry, remove all the excess visible skin
  • Include more vegetables and beans into your cooking
  • Swap Frying or Roasting your food with grilling, baking, roasting or steaming them
  • To manage the amount of oil you use, measure it with a teaspoon or use an oil spray.
  • Choose leaner, lower-fat meats when choosing your meat or poultry
  • Try low-fat spreads like those made with olive or sunflower oils.
Saturated fat is found in:
  • Ghee, butter, lard, coconut oil and palm oil
  • Cakes
  • Biscuits
  • Milkshakes
  • Fatty cuts of meat
  • Sausages
  • Chocolate and chocolate spreads
  • Cured meats like pancetta, salami, chorizo
  • Cheese
  • Pastries, such as pies, quiches, sausage rolls and croissants
  • Sour cream and cream
  • Ice cream
  • Bacon
  • Coconut cream and coconut milk
