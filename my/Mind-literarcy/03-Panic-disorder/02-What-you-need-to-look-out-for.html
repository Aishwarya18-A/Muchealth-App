WHAT DO YOU NEED TO LOOK OUT FOR?
  • Are you frequently sweating lately?
  • Do you experience any trembling or shaking episodes?
  • Do you experience sudden shortness of breath?
  • Do you experience a sudden sense of choking?
  • Do you feel nauseated?
  • Do you tend to feel dizzy or lightheaded frequently?
  • Do you have a sense of feeling unreal or disconnected from yourself?
  • Do you have a fear of losing control?
  • Do you have a fear of "going crazy" or dying?
  • Do you experience sudden chills or hot flashes?
  • Do you have frequent episodes of chest pain and other symptoms that mimic a heart attack?
