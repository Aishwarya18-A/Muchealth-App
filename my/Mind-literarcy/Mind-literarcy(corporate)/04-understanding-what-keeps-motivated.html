UNDERSTANDING WHAT KEEPS EMPLOYEES MOTIVATED AT WORK
    HERE ARE SOME TIPS ON HOW TO KEEP YOUR ORGANIZATION MOTIVATED AT WORK
    1. HAVE A BALANCE AMOUNT OF TEAM MEETINGS AND DO NOT OVERWHELM THEM
    2. EMPHASISE THAT ITS IMPORTANT THAT EMPLOYEES TAKE CARE OF THEMSELVES - SHOW THEM YOU CARE!
    3. PRACTICE GOOD OPEN COMMUNICATION & FEEDBACK SYSTEMS AT WORK
    4. KNOW WHO YOU EMPLOYEES ARE
    5. EXPRESS GRATITUDE AND SAY THE “MAGIC WORDS”!
    6. PRACTICE A GOOD AMOUNT OF FLEXIBILITY FOR YOUR EMPLOYEES AT WORK
    7. HAVE GOOD REWARD AND APPRECIATION SYSTEMS IN PLACE
    8. ENCOURAGE PARTICIPATION AND BE OPEN TO OPINIONS AND FEEDBACK FROM EMPLOYEES
