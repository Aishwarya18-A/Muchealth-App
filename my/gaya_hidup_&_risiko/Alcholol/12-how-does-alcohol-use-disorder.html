HOW DOES ALCOHOL USE DISORDER AFFECT ME?

Excessive alcohol use might be harmful to your health. It's related to:

  • Suicide, depression, and despair
  • Cause breast, liver, colon and mouth cancers.
  • Blackouts, attacks, DUIs, and even homicide are all possibilities.
  • Dementia and other types of brain injury
  • Fetal alcohol syndrome (FAS) (This occurs during exposure to alcohol before birth)
  • Cirrhosis, hepatitis, and fatty liver are all examples of liver issues.

Additionally, heavy or frequent drinking might cause personal issues like:

  • Financial problem
  • Work issue
  • Personal relationships
