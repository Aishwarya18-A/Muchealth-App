WHAT ARE SATURATED FATS?

The majority of them originate from foods that are consumed by animals, such as meat and dairy products, as well as a few plant-based foods like palm and coconut oils.

Foods high in saturated fats
  • Coconut oil and coconut cream
  • Fatty cuts of meat
  • Meat products, including sausages and pies
  • Biscuits, cakes, and pastries
  • Butter, ghee, and lard
  • Cheese, particularly hard cheese like cheddar
  • Cream, soured cream and ice cream
  • Palm oil
  • A few savoury snacks, like cheese crackers and some popcorns
  • Chocolate-flavoured sweets
