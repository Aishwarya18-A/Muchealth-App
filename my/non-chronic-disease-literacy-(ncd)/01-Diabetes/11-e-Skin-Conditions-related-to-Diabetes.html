Skin Conditions related to Diabetes?

Skin complications from uncontrolled sugar levels are usually the first visible sign of diabetes. Proper management of blood sugar levels is usually the best way to prevent and treat skin problems that are due to diabetes. High levels of blood sugar will cause you to urinate more leading to dehydration and dry skin.

High blood sugar can lead to dehydration, dry skin, and inflammation

What do you need to look out for?
  1. Are you having sudden dry skin?
  2. Did you notice any dark patches of skin? mainly near skin folds?
  3. Are you experiencing frequent bacterial infections over the eyelids? such as styes or boils?
  4. Are you experiencing severe itching over your skin?
