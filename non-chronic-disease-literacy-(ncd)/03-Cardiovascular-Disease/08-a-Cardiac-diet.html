How does Blood Pressure Change with Age?

The cardiac diet emphasizes foods such as vegetables, whole grains, and oily fish. These foods are known to be healthy and beneficial for the health of our heart. Besides this we also talk about the importance of limiting certain foods that high in sugar and salt that causes high risks of heart diseases.

Foods to EAT more:
  1. Oily Fish
    • Contains Omega-3 Fatty Acids which have anti-inflammatory properties that are known to keep the heart healthy
    • Examples of fish include (Salmon, Sardines, Mackerel)
  2. Whole Grains
    • Whole grains contain more fibre than refined grains. Examples of whole grains include (whole grain bread, pasta, and rice)
  3. Seeds and Nuts
    • One of the best diets for maintaining good heart health is consuming 2-3 cups seeds and nuts daily.
    • A person can try adding the following to their diet:
    • Example of nuts (Un-salted): walnuts, almonds, Brazil nuts, hazelnuts, pecans, and cashews
    • Example of seeds: sunflower seeds, pumpkin seeds, flaxseeds, hemp seeds, and chia seeds.
  4. Fruits and Vegetables
    • These group of foods are rich in antioxidants that are known to protect and maintain good heart health
